DROP YOUR OWN PHOTOS HERE
=========================

1. Copy your photo files (.jpg / .png) into this folder.
2. Open  js/i18n.js  and add an entry to the GALLERY array, e.g.:

     { src: "assets/images/uploads/pool-sunset.jpg", orient: "landscape" },

   Use "landscape" for wide photos and "portrait" for tall ones.
3. Refresh the site — your photo now appears in the gallery and lightbox.

Tip: keep photos under ~400 KB each (resize to ~1600 px wide) so the
site stays fast to load.
