The House

Two houses, one private paradise

Casa Trini is made up of two fully independent buildings that share a vast enclosed garden — offering privacy and flexibility for families and groups of up to eight guests.

White walls contrast with rustic stone, wooden beams and classic rope chairs, capturing the authentic charm of traditional Formentera. Every corner invites you to slow down and live the island at its own gentle rhythm.
